Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team.

The Man,The Myth,The Legend

◦ Cristiano Ronaldo , was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal .

◦ He plays as a forward player for every team.

◦ Cristiano Ronaldo often considered as the best player in the world and widely regarded as one the greatest player of all time.

◦ He started his youth career in 1992 from Andorinha Club,then in 1995 he moved to Nacional Club and then in 1997 he shifted to Sporting CP.

◦ In 2003 he joined Manchester United, which helped earn him the nickname “CR7.”

◦ Among those successes he became the first football player to score more than 800 official goals.

◦ He also play's for national team called Portugal.

◦ Ronaldo made his senior international debut for Portugal in 2003 at the age of 18 and had since earned over 180 caps.

◦ For playing Sporting CP Ronaldo won the series of Supertaca Candido de Oliveria .

◦ In 2003 Ronaldo joined Manchester United and won 3 Premier League title, 1 FA Cup title, 2 Football League Cup title, 1 UEFA Champions League title and 1 FIFA Club World Cup title.

◦ Ronaldo join Real Madrid in 2009. He won 2 La Liga title, 4 UEFA Champions League title for Real Madrid, 3 FIFA Club World Cup title, and 2 UEFA Super Cup title.

◦ In 2018, Ronaldo joined Juventus and won 2 Series A title, 1 Coppa Italia .

◦ Rather than Club titles he won many other Individual titles also.

◦ Ronaldo has total 4 Golden Shoe Awards till now.

◦ Rather than football Ronaldo has his own hotels and brands which includes Demin's,Perfume's, InnerWear's,EyeWear's and FootWear's.

◦ Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the highest paid Athlete and the first person in the world to reach 500 million followers on social media.

◦ Ronaldo has won 5 FIFA Ballon d'Or . This is the title that every football player want's in his career.
